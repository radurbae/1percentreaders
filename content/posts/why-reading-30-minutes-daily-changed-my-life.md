## The Simple Habit That Compounds

When I started reading just 30 minutes a day, I didn't expect much. But after three months, the compounding effect was undeniable.

### What Changed

1. **Clarity of thought** — My ability to articulate ideas improved dramatically.
2. **Better sleep** — Replacing screen time with reading before bed made a huge difference.
3. **New perspectives** — Each book opened a window into someone else's experience.

### The Math

- 30 minutes × 365 days = **182.5 hours** of reading per year
- That's roughly **25-30 books** depending on your pace
- Over 5 years, that's **150 books** worth of knowledge

> "A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies. The man who never reads lives only one." — George R.R. Martin

### My Advice

Start small. Don't aim for 2 hours a day. Just commit to 30 minutes. The consistency matters more than the duration.

The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is _now_.
