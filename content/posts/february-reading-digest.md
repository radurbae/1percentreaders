## February 2026 Reading Digest

Here are this month's top picks from the 1% Readers community.

### 📚 The List

1. **"Thinking, Fast and Slow"** by Daniel Kahneman
   - _Why:_ Essential for understanding how we make decisions
   - _Best for:_ Anyone curious about psychology and biases

2. **"Meditations"** by Marcus Aurelius
   - _Why:_ Timeless Stoic wisdom in bite-sized entries
   - _Best for:_ Daily reflective reading

3. **"Deep Work"** by Cal Newport
   - _Why:_ A blueprint for focused, meaningful work
   - _Best for:_ Knowledge workers and creators

4. **"The Art of Learning"** by Josh Waitzkin
   - _Why:_ Meta-learning from a chess prodigy turned martial artist
   - _Best for:_ Lifelong learners

5. **"Sapiens"** by Yuval Noah Harari
   - _Why:_ Reframes everything you thought you knew about humanity
   - _Best for:_ Big-picture thinkers
